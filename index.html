Low-latency Deep Analog Audio Transmission using Joint Source Channel Coding

This page provides audio samples corresponding to the simulation results in the paper titled "Low-Latency Deep Analog Audio Transmission using Joint Source-Channel Coding." The audio samples are drawn from two sets of simulation results: comparison with baselines and performance over wireless channels. There are two types of audio samples, one male and one female. First, the clean version is presented, followed by the system's performance for R = 1, SNR = ∞.
